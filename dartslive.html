WHAT IS DARTSLIVE?

This is the website of DARTSLIVE, a service using the network darts machine DARTSLIVE and the designated IC card DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD. By inserting the DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD into DARTSLIVE and playing the game, you can check your data on your PC anytime.

ABOUT DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD

  • Check your results and records on your PC!
  • Compete with other players through rankings!
  • Your name and messages are displayed on the game screen!
  • Exchange information with your friends on the website!

You can enjoy other DARTSLIVE services by logging in to your own card page.


* Registration using the DARTSLIVE PASS is required before viewing the card page.

* You can use all features for free during 15 days free-trial period after logging in to the card page for the first time.

ABOUT DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD

DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD is an IC Card which records your dart play information. You can buy the card from DARTSLIVE shops. You can continue using one card or own several cards for various play purposes such as competition, casual play, league and etc. Buy the DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD and login to the card page!

HOW TO ACCESS THE CARD PAGE

Click HERE to open the card page

  • Click "LOGIN" button to view your card information.
  • If you have not sign up before then click "NEW REGISTRATION" button on card page.
    Enter the necessary information, such as the 16-digit card ID on the back of your DARTSLIVE MEMBERS CARD and PIN Number.

CARD PAGE

On your card page, you can check your performance records or your friends' game results, and customize your game screen, which is helpful for improving your darts skills and exchanging information with other players you have played against. Currently provided features on the card page are shown below:

1. TOP (CARD DATA)

Your basic performance records are displayed. You don't need a notepad or calculator anymore.

  • CRICKET, 01 GAMES (FLIGHT, RATING, STATS)
  • COUNT-UP (HIGH-SCORE)
  • CURRENT MONTH'S EVENT (HIGH-SCORE)
  • DARTSLIVE THEME (CURRENTLY SELECTED THEME, THEME OBTAINMENT HISTORY)
2. RECENT GAME RESULTS

On the RECENT GAME RESULTS page, you can check your past one week play information.

  • STATS
  • AWARD
3. MONTHLY DATA

Monthly average performance records per game category are displayed on the bar graph.

  • 01 GAMES
  • CRICKET
  • COUNT-UP
4. AWARD RECORD

You can check the number of times you hit the AWARDS and BULL'S EYE in the past.

5. GROUP MENU

GROUP MENU offers features such as exchanging information within a group, IN-GROUP RANKING, "WHERE NOW?" for showing where your members are playing, "Where to Go?" to show the shop you will go today, and GROUP INTRODUCTION for registering group members. You can practice secretly by disabling "WHERE NOW?" function.

6. HOME SHOP

You can register your favourite shop as HOME SHOP to check your home information, such as in-shop ranking and players playing in the shop. Notices from your HOME SHOP are also displayed.

7. MY AWARD

You can register awards with your own messages.

8. USER SETTINGS

You can change the name of your card, register your home shop, check your card information, and copy card information.
